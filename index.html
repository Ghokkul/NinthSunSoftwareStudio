How the studio works

From spark to standalone orbit

We're not an agency and not a fund. We're the founding team — engineering, design, and capital under one roof — for every venture we start.

/01

Spark

Six weeks of brutal validation. We prototype the riskiest assumption first, put it in front of real users, and kill anything that doesn't burn on its own.

/02

Orbit

Surviving concepts get a dedicated pod — senior engineers, a designer, and a product lead — shipping weekly until the venture finds paying gravity.

/03

Ignition

We spin the venture out with its own team, cap table, and roadmap. The studio keeps a stake; the sun keeps burning without us.

One constellation, three suns

How the three connect

Cipherscope, Tracelight, and the Prompt Immune System aren't three separate bets — they're one security loop. Each layer feeds the next.

01 — Know

Cipherscope

The knowledge layer. A trusted index of CVEs, advisories, tools, and research that both humans and our AI systems search — the single source of verified truth for the whole stack.

→

02 — Detect

Tracelight.ai

The detection layer. Tracelight's AI analyst hunts anomalies in your logs and queries Cipherscope's index in real time to match what it sees against known CVEs and threat actors — explaining every alert with cited, trusted context.

→

03 — Defend

Prompt Immune System

The protection layer. It shields the AI agents themselves — including Tracelight — from injected instructions, and every attack it quarantines is fed back into Cipherscope's index as fresh, searchable threat intelligence.

Cipherscope informs Tracelight · Tracelight is guarded by Immune · Immune's catches enrich Cipherscope — a closed loop that gets smarter with every threat.

In plain terms

It's not a line. It's a wheel.

Picture it less like a factory line with a start and an end, and more like a wheel that never stops turning. There's no final step where the process shuts off.

Cipherscope hands Tracelight trusted facts to check against. Tracelight uses those facts to catch trouble and hands anything suspicious to the Immune System. The Immune System neutralizes it — and instead of throwing that knowledge away, feeds it straight back into Cipherscope.

Which means the wheel arrives back where it started, except a little wiser than the last time around. Round and round, every threat it catches makes the next lap smarter.
